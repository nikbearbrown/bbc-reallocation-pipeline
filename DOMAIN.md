# BBC Reallocation Pipeline — Domain Index

## Purpose
Bear Brown LLC (BBC) is a supervised AI contracting shop and talent pipeline. Fellows are Humanitarians AI OPT students working 20 hours/week on research. Up to 20 additional hours are available for 1099 contract work through BBC. BBC bills ~$40/hr, fellow receives ~$25/hr. The spread covers oversight, quality guarantee, and BBC's Upwork/platform reputation.

## The Core Thesis
Every contract engagement is a paid interview. If a fellow performs, the startup hires them directly. BBC takes zero friction on conversion. The system exists to find funded startups with real hiring intent, not just clients shopping for cheap labor.

## Fellow Economics
- 20 hours: Humanitarians AI volunteer research (covered)
- Up to 20 hours: 1099 contract via BBC at $25/hr
- BBC bills: ~$40/hr after platform fees
- BBC net margin: ~$15/hr covers oversight, platform costs, reputation risk

## Platform Channels
1. Upwork — wide funnel, noisy, good for portfolio and early-stage screening
2. Braintrust — higher signal, enterprise buyers, avg project $81K/8 months
3. AI Tinkerers — community-driven, warm intros, highest hire-intent signal
4. Latent Space / MLOps Community — deep stack fellows only, practitioner buyers
5. YC / Warm Intro Networks — funded startups, maximum hire-intent, pre-trust
6. Scale AI / Outlier / Mercor — eval supplier track, BBC as coordinated team
7. Combiner — cross-channel orchestration, feeds BBC_DAILY_BRIEF
